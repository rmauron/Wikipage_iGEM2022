Engineering

To achieve our goal of engineering a recombinant Intrinsic Factor (BiG-IF), mutants were designed based on the rationale of preserving structure as much as possible and screened using different in-silico modelling techniques to be tested in wet lab experiments. For which the corresponding primers were designed for site-directed mutagenesis (SDM).

Engineering

Model

No successful wet lab experiment can be performed without proper modelling. To get insights into how desired mutations will affect the structure of IF and its binding properties with B12 and cubilin, a multistep modelling pipeline was performed. Starting with protein structure predictions of all proposed mutants to docking and immunogenicity studies.

Software

Introducing our very own ProMutor - Protein Mutant Generator A user-friendly web tool for selecting potent mutants of your choice. Given a nucleotide or protein sequence, the tool provides a list of mutants ranked based on their effects on protein structure, as well as predicting their potential effect from clinical data.

Wikipage

Priority has been the user-friendliness experience and accessibility of the information. The pages organized in a logical order are accessible by using the menu with only 1 click or by reading the wikipage from start to end using the “previous/next” buttons at the bottom of every page. The side menu allows the user to navigate through the page or simply jump to a specific section of interest. The non-overwhelming organization of the references into a dropdown allows the most agreeable experience. "Inclusive and accessible" is the page's motto primarily achieved by the pleasant and sober font style, the color choice that allows colorblind people to detect shapes in both dark and light modes, particular font style and size make the wiki dyslexic-friendly and descriptive figure captions allow for screen-reader usage. The dynamic elements integrated successfully add life to the wikipage which keeps the user entertained!
